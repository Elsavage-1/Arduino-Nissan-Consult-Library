Information for DIY projects

If you'd prefer to build your own here are some details to get you started. Information is provided for personal (NON-PROFIT) use only. Please note that we do not supply parts in kit form. The printed circuit boards use surface mount components for all but one device. These can be difficult to obtain in small quantities and often present assembly difficulties for the average DIY enthusiast.
